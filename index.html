About


We are a UK non-profit organisation with one goal in mind! Save our bees.


As people go about their busy daily lives, we tend to forget about some of the most pressing issues facing our existence today. Bees fall into this unfortunate category or future uncertainty, not just to them as a species, but also to us as human beings.


Almost 90% of wild plants and 75% of leading global crops depend on animal pollination. One out of every three mouthfuls of our food depends on pollinators such as bees. Crops that depend on pollination are five times more valuable than those that do not.


To put it simply... we cannot live without bees.
